Discount Calculator

Welcome to the Discount Calculator! This tool allows you to calculate discounts based on your age. Simply enter the item's price and your age, and it will provide you with the applicable discount percentage.

- If you're 21 years old or younger, you'll receive a 10% discount.

- If you're 65 years or older, you're entitled to a 5% discount.

- If you fall between these age groups (22 to 64), you won't receive any specific discount.

During the joyous occasion of Durga Puja, we're running a special campaign that provides exclusive sales for both youngsters and senior citizens. Our calculator will help you determine the final price and the discount you can enjoy during this festive season.

Feel free to enter the item's price and your age below and let the Discount Calculator do the rest. Happy shopping!
